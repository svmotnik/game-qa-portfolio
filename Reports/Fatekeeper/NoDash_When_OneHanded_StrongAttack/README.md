__ID__: QA-0002

__SUMMARY__: Dash don't work when while performing Strong Attack Hold with one-handed weapons.

__SEVERITY__: Minor

__PLATFORM__: PC

__BUILD__: 0.1.2

__ENVIRONMENT__:
- i7-8700
- NVIDIA RTX 3060 Gainward Ghost
- 24GB RAM HyperX Fury 2666Hz
- SSD
- Graphics set to max with DLSS on Quality

__EXPECTED__: While two-handed weapon is equipped, dash is performed while Strong Attack is held, cancelling it.

__ACTUAL__: While one-handed weapon is equipped, dash is not performed at all.

__STEPS__:
1. Equip two-handed weapon.
2. Charge Strong Attack.
3. Dash while still holding Strong Attack.
4. Dash is performed while canceling Strong Attack.
5. Equip one-handed weapon.
6. Charge Strong Attack.
7. Dash while still holding Strong Attack.
8. Dash is not performed at all.
